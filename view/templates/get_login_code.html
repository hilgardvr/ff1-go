FF1 Go

Fantasy F1 Game

Welcome to the world of Fantasy Formula 1!

How does it work? You'll manage your own racing team and compete against other players from around the world.

Step 1: Choose a team name

Step 2: Assemble a team by using your budget to buy the 4 drivers that you think will do best in the upcoming race.

Step 3: Create or join a league with friends, colleagues and family. When creating a league you will receive a league password by email that you can share with others to join your league.

Step 4: You need to select 4 drivers every race week 1 hour before qualifying starts.

With each race, you'll earn points equal to the points the drivers in your team earned during that race weekend(including sprint qualifying points & fastest lap points).
