FF1 Go

Fantasy F1 Game

Welcome to the world of Fantasy Formula 1!

How does it work? In this exciting game, you'll get to build your own racing team and compete against other players from around the world.

Step 1: Choose a team name and assemble a team by using your budget to buy the 4 best drivers. You need to select 4 drivers every race week before qualifying starts.

Step 2: Create or join a league with friends, colleagues and family. When creating a league you will receive a league password by email that you can share with others to join your league.

With each race, you'll earn points equal to the points the drivers in your team earned during that race weekend(including sprint qualifying points).

So what are you waiting for? Get behind the wheel and experience the thrill of Fantasy Formula 1 today!
